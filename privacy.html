ShotForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

ShotForge is designed with privacy in mind. All training data, firearm information, and performance statistics are stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

ShotForge does not collect or transmit firearm data, training history, or personal information to any server.

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple's App Store. ShotForge does not store payment information.
